LPU Online & Distance Education in Punjab

LPU Distance Education in Punjab and LPU online courses offer students and working professionals across the state access to world-class higher education from one of India's top-ranked universities. Lovely Professional University, ranked 5th in India by THE Rankings 2026 and NAAC A++ graded, provides UGC-DEB approved online and distance learning programs that are affordable, flexible, and industry-relevant.

Whether you're a working professional in Jalandhar seeking an online MBA, a student in Ludhiana looking for an online BCA or BBA degree, or anyone in Punjab wanting quality distance education — LPU's online & distance programs are designed for you. With fees starting from just ₹7,500 per semester, LPUDE is one of the cheapest distance MBA options in India while maintaining top quality.

Our Punjab admission center provides complete support — from free career counseling and program selection to document verification, fee payment assistance, and exam guidance. MBA for working professionals, online degree programs, and all LPU distance courses are available for students from Jalandhar, Ludhiana, Amritsar, Patiala, and all of Punjab.
