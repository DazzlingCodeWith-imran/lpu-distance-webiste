Everything you need to know about LPU Distance Education in Punjab

Yes, LPU Distance Education programs are fully available across Punjab through our authorized admission center. Students from Jalandhar, Ludhiana, Amritsar, Patiala, and surrounding areas can enroll in all UGC-DEB approved programs including MBA, MCA, BBA, BCA, B.Com, M.Com, and more.

The LPU Distance MBA fee is ₹16,500 per semester, totaling ₹66,000 for the complete 2-year program (4 semesters). This makes it one of the most affordable MBA programs available in Punjab.

Yes, all LPU Distance Education degrees are approved by UGC-DEB (University Grants Commission — Distance Education Bureau) and are valid for government jobs, higher education, promotions, and all official purposes across India.

LPU Distance Education in Punjab offers 23+ programs: MBA (12 specializations), MCA (5 specializations), M.Com, M.Sc. IT, MA (English, Hindi, History, Punjabi, Sociology, Education, Economics, Political Science, Mathematics), MLIS, BBA, BCA, B.Com, B.Sc. IT, BA, BLIS, DBA, DCA, and DLIS.

Applying from Punjab is simple: Fill the application form online or visit our center at Jalandhar-Delhi GT Road, Phagwara, submit your documents (marksheets, ID proof, photos), pay the semester fee, and receive your admission confirmation. We offer free counseling to help you choose the right program.

Eligibility varies by program: PG programs (MBA, MCA, M.Com, MA) require graduation. UG programs (BBA, BCA, B.Com, BA) require 10+2. Diploma programs (DBA, DCA, DLIS) require 10+2. Contact us for specific program eligibility details.

Yes, LPU provides placement assistance to all distance education students. With 2225+ recruiters and 6000+ Fortune 500 offers, LPU has a strong placement record. Students from Punjab can access the same placement support.

Our admission center is located at Jalandhar-Delhi GT Road, Phagwara, Punjab 144411. We serve students from Jalandhar, Ludhiana, Amritsar, Patiala, and all across Punjab with complete admission assistance and free counseling.
