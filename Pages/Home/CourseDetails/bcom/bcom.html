Program Overview

The Distance B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce) program from Lovely Professional University is a comprehensive 3-year undergraduate degree designed for students who want to build a strong foundation in commerce, accounting, taxation, and business studies. Approved by UGC-DEB, the program prepares students for careers in accounting, banking, taxation, finance, business management, and corporate sectors.

The curriculum covers Financial Accounting, Business Law, Taxation, Auditing, Corporate Accounting, Economics, Business Management, Corporate Governance, and Financial Management , helping students develop financial knowledge, analytical abilities, and business decision-making skills required in today's competitive commerce industry.

With the flexibility of distance learning, students can earn a professional commerce degree without attending regular classes. The program is ideal for 12th pass students, aspiring accountants, banking professionals, finance executives, and business enthusiasts . Backed by NAAC A++ accredited LPU and the LPU e-Connect digital learning platform, students receive quality education through experienced faculty, recorded lectures, digital study material, online assessments, and complete learner support. Graduates can also pursue professional courses like CA, CS, or CMA for advanced career opportunities.
