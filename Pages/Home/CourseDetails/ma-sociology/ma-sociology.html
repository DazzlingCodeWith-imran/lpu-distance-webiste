Program Overview

The Distance M.A. Sociology program from Lovely Professional University is a comprehensive 2-year postgraduate degree designed for graduates who wish to develop a deeper understanding of society, social institutions, and human behavior. Approved by UGC-DEB, the program builds strong theoretical and practical knowledge for careers in social research, public administration, education, NGOs, and community development.

The curriculum covers Sociological Theory, Indian Society, Social Stratification, Gender Studies, Urban & Rural Sociology, Social Change, Cultural Dynamics, and Social Research Methods, enabling students to understand complex social issues, conduct research, and analyze contemporary social trends with a critical perspective.

With the flexibility of distance learning, students and working professionals can continue their education without interrupting their careers. The program is especially beneficial for civil services aspirants, social workers, researchers, educators, and NGO professionals. Backed by NAAC A++ accredited LPU, affordable semester fees of ₹8,000, and the LPU e-Connect digital learning platform, learners receive quality education through expert faculty, interactive online resources, and comprehensive study materials.
