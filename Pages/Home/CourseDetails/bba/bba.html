Program Overview

The Distance BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) program from Lovely Professional University is a comprehensive 3-year undergraduate degree designed for students who want to build a strong foundation in business administration, management principles, leadership, and entrepreneurship. Approved by UGC-DEB, the program prepares students for careers in corporate organizations, startups, banking, finance, marketing, human resources, and operations management.

The curriculum covers Marketing Management, Financial Management, Human Resource Management, Business Economics, Accounting, Operations Management, Organizational Behaviour, Entrepreneurship, Business Communication, and Strategic Management, helping students develop practical business knowledge, analytical thinking, leadership abilities, and decision-making skills required in today's competitive business environment.

With the flexibility of distance learning, students can pursue higher education without attending regular classes. The program is ideal for 12th pass students, working professionals, aspiring entrepreneurs, and individuals preparing for management careers. Backed by NAAC A++ accredited LPU and the LPU e-Connect digital learning platform, students receive quality education through experienced faculty, recorded lectures, digital study material, online assessments, and comprehensive learner support.
