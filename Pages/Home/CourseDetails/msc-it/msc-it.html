Program Overview

The Distance M.Sc. IT from LPU is a postgraduate program designed for graduates who want to advance their knowledge in information technology. The UGC-DEB approved curriculum covers advanced topics in software development, networking, database management, and emerging technologies.

This program bridges the gap between theoretical computer science and practical IT applications, preparing graduates for technical and managerial roles in the IT industry. The flexible distance learning mode makes it accessible for working professionals.

LPU's strong industry partnerships ensure that the curriculum stays relevant and graduates receive placement support from 2225+ recruiting partners.
