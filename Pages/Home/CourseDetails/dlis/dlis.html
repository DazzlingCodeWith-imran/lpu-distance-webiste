Program Overview

The Distance DLIS (Diploma in Library & Information Science) program from Lovely Professional University is a 1-year diploma program designed for students who wish to build a strong foundation in library science and information management. Approved by UGC-DEB , the program prepares learners for careers in modern library and information services.

The curriculum covers Library Science Fundamentals, Cataloguing, Classification Systems, Library Organization, Information Services, and Information Management , helping students develop practical knowledge of library operations and documentation processes.

With the flexibility of distance learning, students can continue their education from anywhere without attending regular classes. The program is ideal for 10+2 pass students, aspiring librarians, working professionals, and individuals interested in library and information management . Supported by NAAC A++ accredited LPU and the LPU e-Connect digital learning platform, students receive expert faculty guidance, recorded lectures, digital study materials, online assessments, and continuous academic support. After completing the diploma, students can pursue BLIS (Bachelor of Library & Information Science) for higher qualifications and better career opportunities .
