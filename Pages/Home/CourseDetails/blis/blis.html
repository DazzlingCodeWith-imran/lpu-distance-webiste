Program Overview

The Distance BLIS (Bachelor of Library and Information Science) program from Lovely Professional University is a comprehensive 1-year undergraduate degree designed for graduates who want to build a career in library science, information management, documentation, and digital information services. Approved by UGC-DEB , the program prepares students for professional roles in libraries, educational institutions, research organizations, and information centers.

The curriculum covers Library Organization, Cataloguing, Classification, Information Retrieval, Digital Library Systems, Library Management, Documentation, and Information Services , helping students develop practical knowledge and professional skills required for modern library and information management.

With the flexibility of distance learning, students can complete their degree without attending regular classes. The program is ideal for graduates seeking librarian careers, working professionals, and individuals interested in library and information science . Supported by NAAC A++ accredited LPU and the LPU e-Connect digital learning platform, students receive expert faculty guidance, recorded lectures, digital study materials, online assessments, and continuous academic support. Graduates become eligible for librarian positions in schools, colleges, universities, public libraries, research institutions, and digital information centers .
