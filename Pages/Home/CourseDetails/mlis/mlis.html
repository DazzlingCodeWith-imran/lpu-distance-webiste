Program Overview

The Distance Master of Library and Information Science (MLIS) program from Lovely Professional University is a 1-year postgraduate degree designed for graduates who wish to develop advanced expertise in library management, information services, digital libraries, and knowledge organization. Approved by UGC-DEB, the program prepares students for leadership roles in academic, public, government, and corporate libraries.

The curriculum covers Advanced Library Management, Digital Libraries, Information Retrieval Systems, Research Methodology, Knowledge Organization, Information Sources & Services, Library Automation, and Information Technology, enabling students to efficiently manage modern libraries and digital information systems.

With the flexibility of distance learning, working professionals can continue their education without interrupting their careers. The program is especially suitable for librarians, information professionals, library assistants, researchers, academic staff, and knowledge managers. Backed by NAAC A++ accredited LPU and the LPU e-Connect digital learning platform, students receive quality education through experienced faculty, online study materials, recorded lectures, and interactive learning resources.
