Program Overview

The Distance M.A. English from LPU offers an in-depth study of English literature, linguistics, literary criticism, and critical theory. This UGC-DEB approved program is designed to build advanced knowledge of language, culture, and literary traditions while enhancing analytical and communication skills.

The curriculum includes British Literature, American Literature, Indian Writing in English, Literary Criticism, Linguistics, and Research Methodology. It prepares students for careers in teaching, content writing, publishing, journalism, research, and civil services.

With an affordable fee of just ₹8,000 per semester, the LPU Distance M.A. English program provides quality postgraduate education through a flexible distance learning mode, making it accessible to learners across Punjab and India.
