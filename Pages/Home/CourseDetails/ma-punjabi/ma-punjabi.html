Program Overview

The Distance M.A. Punjabi program from Lovely Professional University is a comprehensive 2-year postgraduate degree designed for students who wish to develop advanced knowledge of Punjabi language, literature, and culture. Approved by UGC-DEB, this program provides a strong academic foundation in classical and modern Punjabi poetry, prose, drama, linguistics, and literary criticism.

The curriculum covers Punjabi Literature, Language Studies, Linguistics, and Cultural Heritage, enabling students to gain expertise in literary analysis, communication, creative writing, and research. Students also develop a deep understanding of Punjabi traditions, folk literature, translation studies, and the evolution of the Punjabi language.

The flexible distance learning mode allows students and working professionals to study at their own pace while balancing professional and personal commitments. The program is especially beneficial for candidates preparing for UGC NET, SET, teaching examinations, and government recruitment. With NAAC A++ accredited LPU, affordable semester fees of ₹8,000, and access to the LPU e-Connect learning platform, students receive quality education supported by experienced faculty and comprehensive digital learning resources.
