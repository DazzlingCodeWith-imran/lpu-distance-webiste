Program Overview

The Distance M.A. Mathematics program from Lovely Professional University is a comprehensive 2-year postgraduate degree designed for mathematics graduates who want to strengthen their knowledge of advanced mathematical concepts and analytical techniques. Approved by UGC-DEB, the program provides a strong academic foundation for careers in teaching, research, higher education, and analytical professions.

The curriculum covers Advanced Algebra, Real Analysis, Complex Analysis, Differential Equations, Numerical Methods, and other advanced mathematical subjects, helping students build logical reasoning, problem-solving, and quantitative analysis skills. The program also prepares learners for academic research and competitive examinations.

With the flexibility of distance learning, students and working professionals can study at their own pace without interrupting their careers. The rigorous curriculum develops strong analytical and critical thinking abilities that are highly valued in education, research, data science, finance, and analytics. Backed by NAAC A++ accredited LPU, affordable semester fees of ₹8,000, and the LPU e-Connect digital learning platform, students receive quality education supported by experienced faculty and comprehensive online study resources.
