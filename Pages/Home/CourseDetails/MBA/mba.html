Program Overview

The Distance MBA program from Lovely Professional University is designed for aspiring business leaders and working professionals who want to advance their careers without leaving their jobs. This UGC-DEB approved program offers 12 in-demand specializations covering Marketing, Finance, HR, Operations, IT, International Business, Supply Chain, Retail, Healthcare, Entrepreneurship, Business Analytics, and Project Management.

The curriculum is industry-aligned and regularly updated to reflect current business trends. Students gain practical knowledge through case studies, projects, and assignments. LPU's distance MBA is recognized by employers across India, with 2225+ recruiting partners offering placement support.

Key highlights include NAAC A++ accreditation, affordable semester fees of ₹16,500, flexible learning through the e-Connect platform, and access to LPU's vast alumni network of 1,50,000+ graduates. The program equips students with strategic thinking, analytical skills, and domain expertise needed to excel in today's competitive business environment.

Exams for LPU Distance MBA are conducted online through the e-Connect platform with secure proctoring technology. Key features include:
