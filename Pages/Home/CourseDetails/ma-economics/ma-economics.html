Program Overview

The Distance M.A. Economics program from Lovely Professional University is a comprehensive 2-year postgraduate degree designed for graduates who want to build expertise in economic theory, data analysis, policy formulation, and research. Approved by UGC-DEB, the program prepares students for careers in banking, government, academia, financial institutions, and economic consulting.

The curriculum covers Microeconomics, Macroeconomics, Econometrics, Development Economics, International Trade, Public Finance, Monetary Economics, and Research Methodology, enabling students to develop strong analytical, quantitative, and decision-making skills for solving real-world economic challenges.

With the flexibility of distance learning, working professionals and competitive exam aspirants can continue their education without interrupting their careers. The program is especially suitable for UPSC aspirants, banking professionals, financial analysts, economists, researchers, teachers, and policy enthusiasts. Backed by NAAC A++ accredited LPU and the LPU e-Connect digital learning platform, students receive quality education through experienced faculty, interactive learning resources, recorded lectures, and comprehensive study material.
