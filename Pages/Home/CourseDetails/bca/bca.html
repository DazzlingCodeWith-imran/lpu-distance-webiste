Program Overview

The Distance BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) program from Lovely Professional University is a comprehensive 3-year undergraduate degree designed for students who want to build a strong foundation in computer science, software development, and modern IT technologies. Approved by UGC-DEB, the program prepares students for careers in software development, web development, database management, cloud computing, and IT support services.

The curriculum covers Programming Languages, Data Structures, Database Management Systems, Web Development, Software Engineering, Computer Networks, Operating Systems, Object-Oriented Programming, Cyber Security, and Mobile Application Development, helping students develop technical, analytical, and problem-solving skills required in today's fast-growing IT industry.

With the flexibility of distance learning, students can earn a professional degree without attending regular classes. The program is ideal for 12th pass students, aspiring software developers, web developers, programmers, and IT professionals. Backed by NAAC A++ accredited LPU and the LPU e-Connect digital learning platform, students receive quality education through experienced faculty, recorded lectures, digital study material, online assessments, and comprehensive learner support. Graduates can also pursue MCA for advanced career opportunities in the IT sector.
