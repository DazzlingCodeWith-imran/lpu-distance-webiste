LPU Distance Education Eligibility 2026 — All Programs

2026-02-12 7 min read By LPU Distance Education Punjab
Share:
Students checking eligibility requirements for admission

Planning to enroll in LPU distance education? Here's a complete breakdown of eligibility criteria for every program available in 2026.

Post-Graduate Programs (PG)

MBA (Master of Business Administration)

  • Eligibility: Bachelor's degree in any discipline from a UGC-recognized university
  • Minimum Marks: 50% aggregate (45% for reserved categories)
  • Age Limit: No upper age limit
  • Work Experience: Not required (but beneficial)
  • Duration: 2 years (4 semesters)

MCA (Master of Computer Applications)

  • Eligibility: BCA / B.Sc. (IT/CS) or any graduation with Mathematics as a subject
  • Minimum Marks: 50% aggregate
  • Age Limit: No upper age limit
  • Duration: 2 years (4 semesters)

M.Com (Master of Commerce)

  • Eligibility: B.Com / BBA or equivalent from a recognized university
  • Minimum Marks: 50% aggregate
  • Duration: 2 years (4 semesters)

MA (Master of Arts)

  • Eligibility: Bachelor's degree in any discipline
  • Minimum Marks: 50% aggregate
  • Subjects Available: English, Hindi, History, Punjabi, Sociology, Education, Economics, Political Science, Mathematics
  • Duration: 2 years (4 semesters)

Under-Graduate Programs (UG)

BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 from any recognized board
  • Minimum Marks: 50% aggregate
  • Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)

BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Mathematics from any recognized board
  • Minimum Marks: 50% aggregate
  • Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)

B.Com (Bachelor of Commerce)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 from any recognized board (Commerce preferred but not mandatory)
  • Minimum Marks: 50% aggregate
  • Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)

BA (Bachelor of Arts)

  • Eligibility: 10+2 from any recognized board
  • Minimum Marks: 50% aggregate (relaxed for some subjects)
  • Duration: 3 years (6 semesters)

Diploma Programs

DBA, DCA, DLIS

  • Eligibility: 10+2 from any recognized board
  • Minimum Marks: 45% aggregate
  • Duration: 1 year (2 semesters)

Important Notes

  1. No entrance exam — All admissions are direct/merit-based
  2. Final year students can apply with provisional admission
  3. Working professionals are welcome — no employer NOC required
  4. Gap years are accepted — no restriction on gap after previous qualification
  5. Reserved categories get 5% relaxation in minimum marks

Documents Required for Admission

  • 10th and 12th marksheets and certificates
  • Graduation marksheets (for PG programs)
  • Aadhaar card / ID proof
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Category certificate (if applicable)
