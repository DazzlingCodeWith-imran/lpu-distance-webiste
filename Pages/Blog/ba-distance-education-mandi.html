LPU's BA program offers a versatile arts curriculum with multiple specializations. The program is designed to equip students with industry-relevant skills that are in high demand across Mandi and all of Himachal Pradesh. The curriculum covers:

  • Skill Development: Communication skills, critical thinking, research methodology
  • Practical Exposure: Project work, case studies, and assignments

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I get a government job with a distance BA degree?

Yes. LPU's BA is UGC-DEB approved, making it fully valid for all government jobs in India, including HPPSC, HPAS, banking, and civil services.

Q: Is BA distance valid for higher education?

Absolutely. You can pursue MA, MBA, or any other postgraduate program from any UGC-approved university after completing your BA from LPU.

Q: How are exams conducted for students in Mandi?

Exams are held at LPU centers across Punjab and Himachal Pradesh. We have dedicated exam centers to serve students from Mandi and surrounding areas.

Q: What are the specializations available in BA?

LPU offers a comprehensive BA curriculum covering English, Sociology, Political Science, History, and Economics. Students can also opt for electives based on their career goals.
