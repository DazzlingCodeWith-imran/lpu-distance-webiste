Payment Options and Financial Planning

LPU understands that many distance education students are working professionals or students from middle-class families. That's why multiple payment options are available to make education accessible:

  • Semester-wise Payment: Pay each semester separately — no need to pay the full amount upfront
  • EMI Options: Available through partner banks and NBFCs, allowing monthly installments
  • Education Loan: Facilitated through nationalized banks (SBI, PNB, BOI) at competitive interest rates
  • Scholarships: Merit-based fee waivers for toppers and category-based concessions
  • Early Bird Discount: Occasional fee waivers for early applications during admission season

For an MBA student paying ₹16,500 per semester, the effective monthly cost is approximately ₹2,750 — less than most monthly mobile phone bills. This makes LPU's distance MBA one of the most financially accessible management programs in India.

What's Included in Fees?

Many students worry about hidden costs. At LPU, the semester fee is all-inclusive:

  • Complete study material (digital + printed textbooks)
  • Online learning platform access with video lectures
  • Recorded video lectures by expert faculty
  • Personal Contact Programme (PCP) participation
  • All examination fees for that semester
  • University registration and enrollment
  • E-library access with thousands of journals and books
  • Student support services and helpdesk access

Pro Tip

The only additional cost you might incur is for re-examination if you fail a subject (₹500-₹1,000 per paper) or for duplicate documents. Regular students who pass all subjects in the first attempt pay nothing beyond the semester fee.

Frequently Asked Questions About Fees

Q: Are there any hidden charges at LPU?

A: No. The semester fee covers everything — study material, exams, LMS access, and registration. The only extra cost is for re-exams or duplicate documents.

Q: Can I pay fees in installments?

A: Yes. EMI options are available through partner banks. You can also pay semester-wise, which spreads the cost over the program duration.

Q: Is the fee refundable if I withdraw?

A: LPU follows UGC refund guidelines. Refunds are available within the stipulated period after admission.

Q: Will fees increase next year?

A: Fees are subject to annual revision, but LPU has historically maintained stable and affordable pricing.

Q: Is education loan available for distance education?

A: Yes. Nationalized banks like SBI and PNB offer education loans for UGC-approved distance programs including LPU.

Important Notes

  • Fees are subject to annual revision by the university
  • Late payment fees may apply after the due date
  • Re-examination fees are separate (₹500-₹1,000 per paper)
  • Contact our team for the latest fee updates and scholarship availability
  • Fee receipts are provided for income tax benefits under Section 80C
