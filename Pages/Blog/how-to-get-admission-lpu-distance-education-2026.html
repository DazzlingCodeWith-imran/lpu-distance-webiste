How to Get Admission in LPU Distance Education — Step by Step Guide

2026-02-13 7 min read By LPU Distance Education Punjab
Share:
Student getting admission guidance with laptop

Getting admission in LPU Distance Education is a straightforward process. This guide walks you through every step — from choosing your program to receiving your enrollment number.

Step 1: Choose Your Program

LPU offers 23+ distance education programs:

Post Graduate:

  • MBA (12 specializations)
  • MCA (5 specializations)
  • M.Com
  • M.Sc. IT
  • MA (8 subjects)
  • MLIS

Under Graduate:

  • BBA
  • BCA
  • B.Com
  • B.Sc. IT
  • BA
  • BLIS

Diploma:

  • DCA
  • DBA
  • DLIS

Tip: Not sure which program? Contact our free counseling helpline for personalized guidance.

Step 2: Check Eligibility

Level Eligibility
PG Programs Bachelor's degree with 50% marks
UG Programs 10+2 with 50% marks
Diploma 10+2 from any board

Note: No entrance exam required. Admission is purely merit-based.

Step 3: Gather Documents

Keep these documents ready (scanned copies for online submission):

  1. ✅ 10th Marksheet & Certificate
  2. ✅ 12th Marksheet & Certificate
  3. ✅ Graduation Marksheet (for PG programs)
  4. ✅ Migration Certificate
  5. ✅ Passport-size Photographs (4-6 copies)
  6. ✅ Aadhar Card / ID Proof
  7. ✅ Caste Certificate (if applicable)

Step 4: Fill the Application Form

Two ways to apply:

Option A: Online Application

  1. Visit our website
  2. Click "Apply Now"
  3. Fill personal details, academic history, and program choice
  4. Upload scanned documents
  5. Submit the form

Option B: Visit Admission Center

  1. Visit our center at Jalandhar-Delhi GT Road, Phagwara, Punjab 144411
  2. Collect physical application form
  3. Fill and submit with document photocopies
  4. Get instant verification

Step 5: Pay the First Semester Fee

Payment modes:

  • UPI: Scan QR code or use UPI ID
  • Credit/Debit Card: Visa, Mastercard, Rupay
  • Net Banking: All major banks supported
  • Demand Draft: In favor of LPU
  • Cash: At admission center

Step 6: Receive Confirmation

After payment, you'll receive:

  1. Admission Confirmation via email/SMS
  2. Enrollment Number — your unique student ID
  3. Login Credentials for the online learning portal
  4. Study Material — shipped to your address within 2 weeks

Timeline

Step Time Required
Application + Documents 1 day
Verification 1-3 days
Fee Payment Same day
Enrollment Number 2-5 days
Study Material Delivery 7-14 days

Important Dates for 2026

  • January Session: Applications open October–January
  • July Session: Applications open April–July
  • Year-round: Contact for current availability
