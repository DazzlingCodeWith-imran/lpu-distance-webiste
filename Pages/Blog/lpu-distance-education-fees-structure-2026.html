What's Included in the Fee?

The semester fee covers:

  • Study Material — Printed Self Learning Material (SLM) books
  • Online Portal Access — Digital study material, recorded lectures
  • Examination Fee — Term-end exam at designated centers
  • Student ID & Registration

Payment Options

  1. Online Payment — UPI, Credit/Debit Card, Net Banking
  2. Demand Draft — In favor of LPU at admission center
  3. Direct Payment — At authorized admission centers

Scholarship & Fee Waivers

  • Merit-based scholarships for toppers
  • SC/ST fee concessions
  • Defence personnel discounts
  • Sibling discount for multiple enrollments

Fee Comparison with Other Universities

LPU's distance education fees are competitive compared to:

  • IGNOU MBA: ₹36,000 (cheaper but limited specializations)
  • Amity Online MBA: ₹1,50,000+ (much more expensive)
  • NMIMS Online MBA: ₹1,25,000+ (significantly higher)
  • LPU Distance MBA: ₹66,000 (best value with NAAC A++ quality)
