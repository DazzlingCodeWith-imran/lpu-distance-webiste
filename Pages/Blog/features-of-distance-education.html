Key Features of Distance Education — How It Works in 2026

2026-02-11 6 min read By LPU Distance Education Punjab
Share:
Student studying online with laptop and books

Understanding how distance education works helps you make an informed decision. Here's a comprehensive look at the key features of distance education from LPU.

Self Learning Material (SLM)

LPU provides comprehensive Self Learning Material — printed books delivered to your address and digital versions accessible through the student portal. Materials are designed for independent study with clear explanations, examples, and self-assessment questions.

Online Student Portal

Access study materials, submit assignments, check exam schedules, view results, and pay fees — all through the LPU student portal (UMS). Available 24/7 from any device.

Personal Contact Programme (PCP)

Face-to-face academic sessions conducted at LPU campus and authorized centers. During PCP, you attend lectures, participate in practicals, interact with faculty, and clarify doubts. Typically 7-15 days per semester.

Examination System

Term-end examinations are conducted at designated centers across India. Exams are held twice a year (June and December). Hall tickets are available on the student portal.

Assignment-Based Assessment

Internal assessment through assignments contributes to your overall grade. Assignments test practical application of concepts and must be submitted before deadlines.

Semester-Based Structure

Programs follow a semester system — study material is organized semester-wise, making it easier to plan your learning schedule.

Flexible Duration

While programs have a minimum duration (2 years for PG, 3 years for UG), students get additional time to complete if needed. MBA can be completed in 2-4 years.

Digital Library Access

Access to e-books, journals, research papers, and online databases through the university's digital library.

Industry-Aligned Curriculum

Curriculum is regularly updated to match industry requirements. Programs include modern topics like digital marketing, data analytics, cloud computing, and more.

Placement Assistance

LPU's placement cell supports distance education students with career services, resume building, interview preparation, and access to placement drives.

Multiple Support Channels

Students can reach support through toll-free helpline (1800-1025-888), email, WhatsApp, and in-person visits to authorized centers.

Conclusion

Distance education from LPU combines traditional learning with modern technology. The structured approach — SLM + online portal + PCP + exams — ensures quality education that fits your lifestyle. Contact our Punjab center for detailed guidance.
