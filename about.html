LPU Distance Education Overview — Punjab

Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a premier institution established by the Lovely Group. The university is recognized by UGC and approved by AICTE, BCI, PCI, NCTE and other regulatory bodies. Students from Jalandhar, Ludhiana, Amritsar, Patiala and across Punjab can access LPU's distance education programs through our authorized admission center.

LPU's Distance Education wing, approved by UGC-DEB (Distance Education Bureau), offers flexible and accessible higher education to students across India and abroad.

With industry-focused curriculum, online learning support, placement assistance and affordable fees, LPU Distance Education has become one of the preferred choices for students across Punjab.

Our Story

A decade of connecting students to opportunity

LPU Distance was founded to close the gap between ambitious students and the accredited distance-learning programs that can change their lives. We partner directly with recognized universities to simplify admission, financing, and support — so students can focus on learning, not paperwork.

Today, we work with 50+ partner universities across the globe, guiding over 15,000 students toward degrees they can earn from anywhere.
